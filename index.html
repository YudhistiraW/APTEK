Biography

Studying Water & Structures to Serve Society

— ◆ —

I am a water building construction engineering student at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember, specializing in hydraulic and water resources engineering. I am passionate about understanding how water moves through our landscapes — and how we can work with it, not against it.

From flood risk assessments to irrigation system design, I approach every project with rigorous analysis and a commitment to sustainable, people-centered solutions. Engineering, to me, is a responsibility as much as it is a discipline.
